New Foundations Explorer   
New Foundations (Wikipedia [external], Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy [external]) is an alternative set theory to the Zermelo-Frankel set theory presented in the regular Metamath Proof Explorer. Unlike ZFC, New Foundations is a direct derivative of the set theory originally presented in Principia Mathematica.
